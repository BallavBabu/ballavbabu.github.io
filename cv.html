Research Profile

I use multi-region input–output models and value-added trade indicators to study how Asia–Pacific production networks connect sectoral growth, structural change, and environmental pressure. My work focuses on South Asia’s integration with China and India through production and income linkages, and on the distribution of embodied emissions and value added across sectors and regions.
